### Quotr Estimation Engine
AI-powered model that predicts construction costs based on historical data, regional pricing, and project specifications.

### Trade Recognition Model
Machine learning system that automatically detects and categorizes subcontractor-specific elements in construction plans.

### MEP Recognition Engine
Specialized AI component that identifies electrical, plumbing, and HVAC components in construction drawings, including symbols and device counts.

### Procurement Graph
Database system that maps relationships between construction materials, suppliers, pricing data, and logistics information.

### Material Confidence Score (MCS)
Numerical indicator (0-100%) showing the AI system's certainty level in its quantity calculations and material recommendations.

Integrated workflow system that automates estimation, takeoff, procurement, and delivery tracking for all construction trades.

### Bill of Materials (BOM)
Complete itemized list of all materials, parts, and components required for a construction project, with quantities and specifications.

### Factory-Direct Sourcing
Procurement method that connects buyers directly with manufacturers, eliminating distributor markups and reducing material costs.
